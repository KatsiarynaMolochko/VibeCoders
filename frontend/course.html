Role of a product manager

How the product manager helps teams deliver value to users.

Instructor: Anna Petrova
Updated on March 10
Certificate upon completion

Learning goals

  • Understand the key tasks of a product manager
  • Learn to measure success through product metrics
  • Explore how to communicate value to stakeholders

Why the product role matters

The product manager connects business goals with user needs. They define priorities, translate insights into features, and coordinate teams to deliver results.

“A product manager is the voice of the user inside the company.”

— Marty Cagan

Key responsibilities

Vision and strategy

Define product goals, roadmap, and priorities together with leadership.

Research

Collect feedback, run interviews, and validate hypotheses.

Working with the team

Formulate tasks, set priorities, and review delivered features.
